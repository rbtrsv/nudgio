# IBKR Stock Trading Strategy

## BUY Signal
- Price < VWAP (or within 0.5% of VWAP)
- Stock RSI < Market RSI - 5 (relatively oversold)
- Stock above 50-day MA (uptrend filter)
- → Place buy order at VWAP or -0.5% below current

## SELL Signal
- Profit ≥ 1.0% from entry price
- → Place sell order at +0.1% above current

## Key Points
- VWAP = Fair value where institutions trade
- Relative RSI = Stock weak vs market (catch-up play)
- 50-day MA = Avoid downtrends
- 1% profit target = Good for stocks (vs 1.6% for crypto)
- Simple and clean like Kraken bot

## Profit Targets by Asset
- Stocks: 1.0%
- Forex: 0.5%
- Crypto: 1.6% (Kraken)
